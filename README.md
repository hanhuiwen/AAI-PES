# Context-Dependent Probabilistic Execution Structures
## Abstract and Research Methodology

---

### Abstract / 摘要

#### English Version
We introduce a **context-dependent probabilistic execution structure** that strictly extends classical probabilistic transition systems. Unlike Segala systems [14], transition probabilities in our model may depend on the execution context, represented as histories.

We establish a **strict non-embeddability theorem**, demonstrating that classical probabilistic transition systems cannot capture such context-dependent behavior. Our research further develops the following:
* **Trace Semantics:** We construct trace semantics via measurable trace spaces in the sense of Giry [4].
* **Coalgebraic Representation:** We prove a final coalgebra representation theorem following standard coalgebraic methodology [13].
* **Minimal Realisation:** We derive a canonical minimal realisation through trace equivalence, yielding a **probabilistic Myhill–Nerode theorem**.

Finally, this work prepares the ground for modal and first-order logical characterisations developed in subsequent sections.

---

#### 中文
我们引入了一种**上下文相关的概率执行结构**，该结构对经典的概率迁移系统进行了严格扩展。与 Segala 系统 [14] 不同，我们模型中的迁移概率可能取决于以历史记录（Histories）为表现形式的执行上下文。

我们建立了一个**严格不可嵌入性定理**，证明了经典的概率迁移系统无法捕捉这种上下文相关的行为。随后，我们开展了以下工作：
* **迹语义（Trace Semantics）：** 我们通过 Giry [4] 意义上的可测迹空间构建了迹语义。
* **余代数表示：** 遵循标准的余代数方法论 [13]，我们证明了一个终结余代数（Final Coalgebra）表示定理。
* **最小化实现：** 我们通过迹等价导出了一个规范的最小化实现，从而得到了一个**概率 Myhill–Nerode 定理**。

最后，本研究为后续章节中展开的模态逻辑及一阶逻辑刻画奠定了基础。
